# Library

To add a game, point GrogVM at a folder holding a copy you own. Nothing is
uploaded — your files stay in the browser — and the game appears below, ready to
play or explore.

GrogVM never bundles or distributes game data; you supply your own copy of a game
you legally own. New here? See **[Legal & game files](/legal/)** for where to get
*The Secret of Monkey Island* legitimately and exactly what GrogVM does and
doesn't ship.
