# Library

To add a game, point GrogVM at a folder holding compatible *The Secret of Monkey
Island* data files from a copy you are allowed to use. Nothing is uploaded —
your files stay in the browser — and the game appears below, ready to play or
explore.

GrogVM never bundles or distributes game data; you supply your own copy. New
here? See **[Legal & game files](/legal/)** for where to get
*The Secret of Monkey Island* legitimately and exactly what GrogVM does and
doesn't ship.
